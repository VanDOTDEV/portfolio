CEBU-BOHOL EDUCATIONAL TOUR 2025

UP CEBU Business Incubator

UP Cebu inIT is the Technology Business Incubator (TBI) of University of the Philippines Cebu (UP Cebu) in partnership with the Department of Science and Technology (DOST).

Observation: This incubator helps new tech businesses get started by giving them advice, resources, and support. It's a place where fresh ideas can grow and turn into real companies.
Learnings: With the right help and resources, new businesses can grow faster. Working together with experts helps them avoid mistakes and become successful more easily.
Dynata Philippines INC.

Dynata is a global data, market research, and survey sampling company. They provide data contributed by consumers and business professionals worldwide.

Observation: Dynata collects lots of information to help businesses understand their customers. They provide real-time data that companies use to make decisions.
Learnings: Having access to up-to-date information helps companies make better choices. Knowing what people think and want is key to making successful products or services.
MATA TECHNOLOGIES

Mata Technologies is a Cebu-based startup creating 360° virtual tours, virtual maps, and VR experiences for tourism, real estate, and other industries.

Observation: Mata Technologies uses virtual reality (VR) to help people experience places without actually being there. This is especially helpful for tourists and people looking to buy property.
Learnings: Virtual reality is a powerful tool for helping people experience things from a distance. It’s a great way to show people what places and products are like before they visit or buy them.
T.A.R.S.I.E.R 117

TaRSIER 117 is Bohol’s emergency response line, a 24/7 dispatch center handling calls for medical emergencies, fires, accidents, and more.

Observation: TaRSIER 117 is a 24/7 service that helps with emergencies, making sure people get help quickly in case of accidents or disasters.
Learnings: Having a fast and reliable emergency service can save lives. The quicker help arrives, the better the outcome for people in need.
RIVAN IT CEBU

Rivan IT Cebu empowers IT professionals, fosters innovation, and uncovers exceptional talent across the evolving tech landscape. Through skill development, growth opportunities, and a dynamic community, we drive progress and shape a transformative future for IT.

Observation: Rivan IT Cebu focuses on cutting-edge fields in IT such as Cyber Security, Network Engineering, and DevOps. By providing skill development and growth opportunities, Rivan IT prepares individuals to thrive in the rapidly evolving tech industry.
Learnings: The IT industry is fast-paced and constantly evolving. Rivan IT Cebu offers a comprehensive learning experience, empowering individuals with the skills they need to succeed in high-demand fields and contribute to the transformation of the tech landscape.
